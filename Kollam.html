FACTS ABOUT KOLLAM

Kollam district is one of the 14 districts of the stateof kerala,India. The district has a crosssection of keralas natural attributes; it is endowedwith a long coastline, a major Laccadive Sea seasport and an inland lake. The district has many water bodies. Kollam is a southern district of kerala,located 70km north to the states capital Thiruvananthapuram.
